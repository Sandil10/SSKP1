Frequently Asked Questions

What services does SSKP GROUP provide?

SSKP offers comprehensive visa consultancy services, including tourist visas, work visas, student visas, and business visas. We provide personalized guidance and support throughout the visa application process to ensure a smooth and successful experience.

How can I apply for a visa through SSKP (Pvt) Ltd?

To apply for a visa, you can contact us via phone, email, or visit our office. Our experienced consultants will guide you through the application process, help you gather the necessary documents, and submit your application to the relevant authorities..

What documents are required for a visa application?

The required documents vary depending on the type of visa you are applying for. Generally, you will need a valid passport, recent photographs, proof of financial stability, travel itinerary, and any specific documents required by the destination country. Our consultants will provide a detailed checklist based on your specific visa type.
